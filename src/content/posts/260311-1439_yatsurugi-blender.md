## 🛠️ Key Skills & Details That Define Professional Quality

![Course Image 2](https://cdn.day1company.io/prod/uploads/202503/140512-1093/portfolio-3dcgmodeler-yatsurugi-jp-02.webp)

Beyond just hitting polycount targets, professional 3D VTuber modeling hinges on translating artistic vision into tangible 3D assets. This course dives deep into the practical techniques Yatsurugi employs:

*   **Topology for Expression:** Understand how edge flow directly impacts facial expression deformation. Yatsurugi demonstrates specific loop structures that allow for nuanced mouth shapes and eye movements.
*   **Weight Painting Precision:** Learn the subtle art of weight painting to ensure clothing and accessories deform naturally with the character's body. This involves careful assignment of bone influences, often down to the vertex group level for fine-tuning.
*   **Efficient UV Unwrapping:** Master UV unwrapping techniques that maximize texture space and minimize distortion. Yatsurugi’s approach focuses on creating clean UV islands that are easy to texture and manage.
*   **VRM Export Best Practices:** Crucial for VTubing, Yatsurugi breaks down the VRM export process, highlighting common pitfalls and how to ensure compatibility and correct rigging for animation and motion capture.
