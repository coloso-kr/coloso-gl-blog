## 💡 3 Core Techniques for a Standout Color Palette

### 1. Understanding the Logic of Soft, Clean Colors
To avoid muddy or dull colors, focus on the subtle interplay of hue, saturation, and brightness. WSFish breaks down how to train your eye to see these relationships, moving beyond mere guesswork. This isn't just about applying a filter; it's about developing a deliberate color control system that shapes the mood and atmosphere of your artwork.

### 2. Mastering Time and Space Through Color Expression
Color is your primary tool for conveying atmosphere. Learn WSFish's method for understanding how light and shadow truly function – that light isn't just white, and shadows aren't just black. By systematically analyzing light sources and WSFish's unique "hidden color" techniques, you can inject richer, more vibrant, and intentional color into your scenes.

### 3. Building Your Signature Soft Tone Style from Rough to Finish
WSFish guides you through a complete workflow, connecting color theory, material expression, compositional elements, and post-processing adjustments. This step-by-step process helps you build a cohesive, stylish look, fostering your individual artistic voice and allowing you to intentionally capture specific tones and atmospheres.
