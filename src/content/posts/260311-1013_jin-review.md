## 💡 Jin's Core Techniques for Killer Fight Scenes

### 1. Master Body Mechanics for Weight and Impact
Instead of just drawing poses, focus on how the entire body supports each action. Jin emphasizes understanding the natural flow of joints and how weight shifts create believable impact. This means **breaking down complex movements into their fundamental mechanical parts**, ensuring each action has a clear cause and effect. Think about how a punch isn't just an arm motion, but a full-body transfer of energy.

### 2. Streamline Workflow with Smart Keyframing
Jin reveals how he identifies and prioritizes keyframes to build a strong foundation for his animation. This involves **spending dedicated time on the most crucial poses** – the start, end, and peak of each motion. By efficiently handling these key moments, you can then focus on the in-betweens and secondary actions without getting bogged down. It’s about working smarter, not just harder.

### 3. Elevate with Dynamic FX Principles
Great fight scenes aren't just about character movement; they're about the environment reacting. Jin teaches how to **create impactful 2D effects like smoke and fire** by understanding their visual principles – how they move, dissipate, and interact with light. This involves using simple, repeatable techniques that add a polished, professional sheen to your work without excessive complexity.
