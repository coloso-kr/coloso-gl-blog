## 💬 Frequently Asked Questions

**Q. Illustrator SENA 8ito mentioned Photoshop Actions. How can I create my own custom actions?**

A. To create your own actions, open the Actions panel in Photoshop (Window > Actions). Click the "Create New Action" button, name your action, and assign a function key shortcut if desired. Then, perform the steps you want to record. Click the "Stop" button when finished. You can then run this action anytime to repeat the recorded steps.

**Q. Illustrator SENA 8ito uses both Photoshop and Clip Studio Paint. Which one is better for illustration composition?**

A. Both programs offer robust tools for illustration. Photoshop excels with its powerful layer management, Smart Objects, and versatile Actions panel, as demonstrated by SENA 8ito. Clip Studio Paint is also excellent, particularly for linework and its drawing-centric tools. The "better" program often depends on your personal workflow and specific needs; SENA 8ito uses them strategically for different tasks.

**Q. Illustrator SENA 8ito demonstrated using Smart Objects. When is the best time to convert a layer to a Smart Object?**

A. It's best to convert layers to Smart Objects early in your workflow, especially if you anticipate needing to resize, transform, or edit the content of that layer multiple times. This preserves the original image data, preventing quality degradation that occurs with repeated rasterized transformations. Using an action to batch convert multiple layers is highly recommended for efficiency.
